## 🌈 Color Recommendations

### By Map
- **Dust2**: Cyan or Purple (avoid yellow/tan)
- **Inferno**: Cyan or Green (avoid orange)
- **Mirage**: Cyan or Green (avoid yellow)
- **Nuke**: Any bright color works
- **Overpass**: Green or Cyan (avoid gray)
- **Vertigo**: Cyan or Pink (avoid blue/gray)
- **Ancient**: Cyan or Pink (avoid green/brown)
- **Anubis**: Cyan or Green (avoid yellow/tan)

### Universal Colors
1. **Cyan** (#00FFFF) - Best overall visibility
2. **Green** (#00FF00) - Classic, works everywhere
3. **Magenta** (#FF00FF) - High contrast, unique
4. **Yellow** (#FFFF00) - Bright, but limited on some maps

## 📱 Resolution Considerations

### 1920x1080 (16:9)
All three crosshairs work perfectly at native size.

### 1280x960 (4:3 Stretched)
- Models appear wider (easier to hit)
- Crosshairs may appear slightly stretched
- Consider reducing crosshair size by 1

### 1280x720 (16:9 Lower Res)
- Everything appears larger
- Reduce crosshair size/thickness if needed
- Dot crosshair works best

### 2560x1440 (1440p)
- Everything appears smaller
- May need to increase crosshair size
- Thin crosshairs (ZywOo) may be too small
